Mero Health

Despite the development of technology the general people are unable to utilise its benifitis in health sector properly. So we developed an Android App which is a complete one stop solution for patient’s appointment, medial record keeping, payment and recomendation of doctors/physicians according to the symptoms they are having. We tried to co-relate the patients’ symptoms and their preferences with the type of doctors they need to visit.

About App:

Following are the technical specifications of our app.

  1. Frontend: Flutter
  2. Backend: NodeJs
  3. Database: MongoDb
  4. Classifier Algorithm: Naive Bayes Classifier
